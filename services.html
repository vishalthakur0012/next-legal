FORMATION OF APARTMENT OWNER’S ASSOCIATION

Though many apartment societies are formed under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, of 1960, it cannot be considered a legally-valid option for the formation of apartment Societies or associations as it does not support the apartment owner’s interest in the property. We can help you in the formation and registration of apartment owners' associations/ societies/ resident welfare associations in Bangalore as well as in different parts of the country. Since Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 is specifically enacted for the administration of apartment complexes, we advise our clients to form associations under the same statute popularly known as KAOA, 1972.
